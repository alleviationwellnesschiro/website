Torque Release Technique

Dr. Bigoms has Certified Advanced Proficiency in Torque Release Technique, which research has demonstrated to be the most specific and reproducible chiropractic technique in the world. Torque Release is the first chiropractic technique of the new millennium and allows practice members of any age to experience the greatest results possible through a gentle approach that does not involve any twisting, “popping,” or “cracking” of the spine. Torque Release Technique was developed as part of scientific research on the health benefits of chiropractic. It is a mixture of the “best-of-the-best” systems of chiropractic assessment and care. Research published in the prestigious journal Nature and Molecular Psychiatry showed a dramatic reduction in anxiety and depression levels and improved measurements of well being through Torque Release Chiropractic care.

Prenatal Care

At Alleviation Wellness Chiropractic, we work with expecting mothers throughout their labor. Dr. Bigoms is a Chiropractor who specializes in care for pregnant women and children. Using the Webster technique, Dr. Bigoms focuses on making the journey of pregnancy a better experience. When coming in for prenatal services, our mothers often experience less pain in their lower backs / hips, shorter delivery times, optimal baby positioning, and less fatigue / nausea. As part of our prenatal chiropractic services, Dr. Bigoms will provide a complementary adjustment to your newborn within the first couple of days of life.

Children Care

At Alleviation Wellness, our motto with kids is, "We'd rather raise healthy kids, as opposed to fixing sick adults." We begin seeing children as early as a few days after they are born all the way up through the teenage years. Chiropractic care has provided many families with a great alternative to traditional drugs and medicines that are being given to our kids at an alarming rate. Dr. Bigoms has seen great results in problems such as, colic, ear infections, ADHD, allergies, bedwetting, sleep issues and acid reflux.

Traumatic Events

There's nothing worse than being on the other end of an accident. At Alleviation Wellness, we specialize in helping restore the functioning of the body to the pre-accident form or better. We utilize a combination of chiropractic care with in-office therapies to ensure that our practice members who come in after a accident are on a great road to recovery. We can work with an attorney or insurance adjuster to ensure that all of your treatment is well documented and delivered for a case if necessary.

Pasqueal Bigoms

Dr. Pasqueal Bigoms is a GA native. Raised in Decatur, he graduated Life University with dual degrees in nutrition and chiropractic. He then worked in offices throughout the Atlanta area, mastering techniques including full spine, torque release technique, and atlas orthogonal. Dr. Bigoms is excited to give back to the place he has called home for the past three years, and looks forward to spreading health and wellness to the community. He is an avid football fan (Go Falcons!), and you can catch him around town with his wife, Shavon, and sons PJ and Miles.
